How to Play

• Survive each Sector by navigating hazards and defeating hostile drones.

• After a Sector, choose 1 of 3 Chimera Blueprints to evolve your ship.

• Stack upgrades to craft builds, then face the Sector Guardian.

• Make it through all Sectors to confront the Singularity.
